Augmented and Virtual Reality: Technology of the future

     The world is advancing faster than it has ever before, and it is tough to keep up with the newest technologies. This blog aims to give you a brief idea of the Virtual and Augmented Reality world in the easiest way possible. 'Augmented Reality' blends chunks of the real and virtual world in desired proportions. It is definitely different from Virtual Reality, the objective of AR(Augmented Reality) is to combine the real interactive world with the in the virtual world which emulates the interactiveness. However, virtual reality allows you to notch up your experience, it creates a virtual world around you where you can literally be a part of a whole different virtual space.

     At present, there are multiple number of wearable and non-wearable devices that are available for the AR/VR experience. The AR/VR market is expected to grow about 8 times its current size in the next 5 years, making it one of the fastest-growing categories in the emerging technologies . With over 171 M current users, the industry is expected to revolutionize the entire way of virtual interactions. By 2025, the net worth of the Augmented and Virtual Reality industry is expected to be $77 billion, growing at a CAGR of 38.1%. It is anticipated that the wearables market will see a massive hike in its worth and by the end of 2025, the wearable market will be worth $116.88 billion. Indeed, established companies can see immense potential in the same, and so do start-ups.

     Key players in the market are Microsoft, Google, Facebook, Apple, Sony, Oculus, HTC, Magic leap, Seiko Epson and Lenovo seems to have a whip hand over the other companies. They have launched products to level up their AR/VR market game and few start-ups like Black-Box VR have entered the booming market as well.A good thing about AR devices is that it doesn't need any dedicated hardware.Our smartphones, laptops, tablets, computers etc. basically a functional screen can serve the purpose.

  • Magic Leap One: This headset is aimed at how medical professionals would work and learn. Not restricting its use only to medical workers, the device has gained popularity amongst other domains. Nevertheless, people are loving it.

     Augmented Reality has seen unprecedented growth in the past 5 years, it is expected to grow exponentially shortly. It is thus worth exploring the intriguing technologies and avenues that drive the market for the future. Although many applications involve AR in shopping, Virtual assistance, navigation, etc. one technology worth mentioning is Mobile AR technology.

This has led to the increased use of Apple's LiDAR hardware. Google's ARCore is introducing publicly made street view photos and Google is now incorporating the photos taken by smartphones having ARCore with Google Maps and the ones taken with Specialized Equipment.

     Unlike Augmented Reality, Virtual Reality takes us to a whole new world wherein we can be our digital selves and interact flawlessly in the digital world. It gives us the advantage to be anyone, do anything, and be anywhere we can possibly imagine. With the rapid increment in the number of users, suppliers, and 5G, it is only a matter of time before the world will have their own digital ones along with their physical bodies.

     This breakthrough technology was brought to us by Facebook and Microsoft. Well, the two most legendary companies investing in something so imaginary and unreal, it must hold much more potential than we can realize at this point in time. When every other blog or company is trying to explain Metaverse, let's try to get a gist of it in Layman's terms, basically it is a world on the internet where we can live digitally or better say in a digital avatar. Although the vision is same for both the companies, their objectives are distinctly different. For Facebook, it is more about social interaction, and for Microsoft, it is work about work. To enable such an experience, the companies are investing hefty amounts in Oculus Headsets. As mentioned, Facebook will allow your avatar to meet, party, play, and whatnot in its form in the Metaverse. Microsoft focuses on the more learning and teamwork side of the technology and it will enable its users to work together in teams digitally in together and presenter mode. Users will be able to access virtual offices and other spaces.

     The future looks surreal and real at the same time. Unimaginable technologies will make it possible to allow us to live entirely different lives in the virtual world.
